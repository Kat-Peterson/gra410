About Me

As an adamant advocate for effective visual communication through the application of the principles of design, I am always eager to take on new design challenges.

I am currently studying as a senior in the Graphic Design and Media Arts program at Southern New Hampshire University, which has allowed me to become proficient in my usage and application of software like the Adobe Creative Suite and Figma. Additionally, my time in school has helped me build supplementary skills in time-management, organization, and creative solutions, allowing me to receive recognition on the Honor Roll and Dean’s List at SNHU. I am currently maintaining a 3.953 GPA, in addition to working on personal projects to better my skills, and I am always eager to contribute and improve where needed.
