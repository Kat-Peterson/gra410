Logo Design: Main Street Dough

These logos were designed for Main Street Dough - a fictional bakery with a fairly young audience and goals of embracing a new, bold look. The geometric imagery and simplistic design work together to create structure, while maintaining a happy, spirited tone.

Typographical Portrait: Bob Ross

This portrait of television artist Bob Ross was produced using a single font, ITC Korinna. The text that makes up the portrait is composed of words describing Ross’s character, famous quotes from his show, and titles of his paintings. In order to create shadows and highlights throughout Ross’s features, I utilized contrast in font size, weight, and color for visual differentiation.

Magazine Article: Wood-Block Printing

This magazine article by The Japanese Method was designed to achieve a sophisticated, elegant look. An image cutout from a Japanese wood-block print piece was used to create a visually dynamic cover page, followed by a second page containing complementary visual elements. Serif fonts were used to increase readability, and further the refined style of the design.

Logo Design: Symplycut Creations

Symplycut, a small laser-cutting business, needed both a primary logo, and a submark logo for their online shops and profiles. This client, with an audience predominantly made up of women ages 16-55, was looking for a contemporary, feminine look for their brand. The resulting logos were composed of black strokes on an off-white background, with floral elements throughout.

Brand Identity: Upper Crust Bakery

Upper Crust Bakery, a fictional French bakery, was in need of a new logo that reflected their values of using generational knowledge to produce quality goods. This client required that their logo be produced in black and white, as well as full color. They also requested the creation of stationery items, such as letterhead and business cards, that included this logo within each design. This design aims to capture a classic, timeless feel, while also drawing on contemporary design elements to reach modern audiences.
